Alcoholics Anonymous Group Google Site
Product Brief & Functional Design
A modern, private, and user-friendly Google Sites template to support AA group communication, resource sharing, and organization—while prioritizing member confidentiality and ease of use.
Purpose
Support, Not Replace: The site is designed to support in-person AA group activities, not replace them.

Confidential & User-Friendly: Prioritizes privacy and ease of use for all members and trusted servants.
Target Users
  • AA Group Members (new & existing)
  • Group Organizers & Trusted Servants
  • Professionals & Public Organizations

Privacy & Security
  • No public member names/photos
  • Anonymous public forms
  • Internal data access restricted to trusted servants
  • Clear data retention and removal policies
  • Outreach maps show only public locations
Maintenance & Success
  • Easy updates by non-technical members
  • Clear instructions for editing/maintenance
  • Regular content reviews for accuracy & privacy
  • Members find info easily
  • Trusted servants work efficiently
  • No public exposure of personal data
  • Public/professional inquiries handled respectfully
