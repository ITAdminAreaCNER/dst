Shared Drive Setup Procedure

A formal, transparent process for creating new Shared Drives.

Operating Procedure: Setting Up a New Shared Drive

Please Note: Shared Drives are a feature exclusive to Google Workspace accounts. Members using a personal Gmail account (@gmail.com) cannot create Shared Drives, which is why this procedure must be initiated by a request to the Area IT Administrator.

1

Request

A trusted servant (e.g., a new DCM or Committee Chair) formally requests a new Shared Drive from the Area IT Administrator, usually via email. The request should specify the purpose and name of the drive (e.g., "District 10 PI/PA Committee").

2

Creation

The IT Administrator creates the Shared Drive using a standard naming convention to keep the workspace organized. They ensure top-level settings are correct.

3

Initial Assignment

The IT Administrator adds the requesting trusted servant to the new drive and assigns them the "Manager" role. This gives the trusted servant full control over the day-to-day management of their drive's membership and content.

4

Handover & Confirmation

The IT Administrator confirms with the new Drive Manager that they have access and understands their role. From this point, the IT Administrator's involvement ceases unless technical support is required. The responsibility for managing the drive now rests with the local service entity.
